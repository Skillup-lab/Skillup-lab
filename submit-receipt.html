Skill Up - User Guide

Everything you need to know as a Student or Teacher

For Students

1. Creating Your Learner Profile: Head to the Learner Registration Page. Fill in your interests, preferred learning method (in-person or online), and your available schedule.

2. Finding a Teacher: Once your profile is complete, our team will match you with verified teachers based on your subject, location, and preferred mode of learning.

3. Booking Classes: Book sessions directly via WhatsApp with your matched teacher. We suggest setting your goals and number of sessions from the start for clarity.

4. Payment Process: You pay your teacher directly — Skill Up does not take any cut at this stage. Always request a receipt after payment.

5. Support & Disputes: Reach out to the Skill Up support team via WhatsApp if you experience issues with your teacher or learning sessions.

6. Learning Tips: Stay consistent, ask questions, and let your teacher know what works best for you. Your feedback helps us improve!

For Teachers

1. Becoming a Skill Up Teacher: Register on the Teacher Registration Page. You'll be asked to list your skills, subjects, rates, availability, and preferred teaching mode.

2. Profile Screening: Our team will verify your submission and contact you to complete onboarding. This may include a quick call or identity confirmation.

3. Getting Matched: Once approved, we’ll begin matching you with students who fit your profile. You’ll be contacted via WhatsApp for each match.

4. Teaching Your Way: You’re free to structure your sessions as you wish. Be clear with students on your availability, fees, and preferred materials.

5. Receiving Payments: Students pay you directly. You are responsible for confirming payment and delivering the agreed sessions.

6. Community Conduct: Respect your learners, maintain professionalism, and communicate clearly. Teachers who get multiple negative reviews may be removed.
