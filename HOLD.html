How Payment Works

This is our MVP. Please follow instructions carefully. Your feedback shapes the main version.

1. Talk to a Teacher First

Before making any payment, reach out to the teacher you're interested in learning from. You can message them directly on WhatsApp from their profile.

2. Receive Payment Instructions

If the teacher confirms availability, we (or the teacher) will share a local bank account number with you via WhatsApp. Payments are done directly, not through the site (for now).

3. Send Proof of Payment

After you complete the transfer, kindly send your payment proof (screenshot or transfer confirmation) via WhatsApp. This helps us or your teacher confirm and schedule your lesson faster.
