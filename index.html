A Welcome Note From the Skill Up Team

Welcome to Skill Up — a grassroots learning platform proudly built in Nigeria, for Nigerians, by Nigerians. We're a passionate team of everyday problem-solvers who saw one simple truth: there are people around us with valuable skills, and others eager to learn — but no bridge connecting them.

This MVP is just the beginning. Our goal is to help you teach what you know, build a trusted brand as a local tutor, and empower others in your community. Whether you’re a teacher or learner, this is your space to grow. Thank you for being part of the Skill Up story.

How It Works

1. You Tell Us What You Need

Whether you're a learner or a teacher — start by filling a quick form. Tell us what you want to learn or teach, your location, budget, and preferred learning style.

2. We Review and Match

We carefully review each submission. Learners get personally matched with trusted teachers. Teachers get matched with real students who fit their skills and vibe.

3. Learning Begins — Your Way

Classes happen on WhatsApp, Zoom, or in-person — it’s up to you. Learners set the pace, and teachers deliver in a way that works best for both sides.

4. Share, Grow, and Build Your Brand

After class, share your experience. Learners help others discover quality tutors and Certificates. Teachers grow their visibility and reputation through results and reviews.

Why Choose Skill Up

1. Built for Nigerians, by Nigerians

We understand your hustle — that’s why we connect you with tutors who truly get your pace, goals, and situation.

2. Verified and Vetted Teachers

Every tutor goes through manual checks — so you’re not just learning from anyone, but someone you can trust.

3. Learn Anything You Want

From JAMB help to digital skills, tailoring, cooking, and even data analysis — Skill Up helps you find your perfect teacher.

4. Learn Your Way

Prefer face-to-face in your area? Want online flexibility? Skill Up works how you want it — even if it's hybrid.
